üîó Technique 1: Chain-of-Thought Prompting

Instead of asking AI to jump to conclusions, guide it through step-by-step reasoning. This is especially powerful for complex decisions or analysis.

Consider how well they match our company culture 4. Finally, weigh the pros and cons before making a recommendation Here's the candidate information: [candidate details]

Real Example - Candidate Evaluation:

Help me evaluate a Marketing Manager candidate. Think through this systematically: 1. Experience Analysis: Review their 5 years in digital marketing 2. Skill Assessment: Evaluate their campaign management experience 3. Cultural Fit: Consider their collaborative approach mentioned in interview 4. Growth Potential: Assess their leadership capabilities 5. Final Recommendation: Provide hire/no-hire with reasoning Candidate Profile: Sarah Chen, 5 years digital marketing experience, led 3 successful product launches, strong in data analysis, collaborative leadership style, seeking growth opportunities.
AI Response: Let me analyze Sarah Chen systematically: 1. Experience Analysis: 5 years in digital marketing shows solid foundation. Leading 3 successful product launches demonstrates project management and results delivery... 2. Skill Assessment: Strong data analysis skills are crucial for modern marketing. Campaign management experience directly relevant... 3. Cultural Fit: Collaborative leadership style aligns well with our team-oriented culture... 4. Growth Potential: Seeking growth opportunities suggests ambition and long-term potential... 5. Final Recommendation: HIRE - Strong technical skills, proven results, cultural alignment, and growth mindset make her an excellent fit.
