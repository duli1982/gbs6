Your First AI Conversation

Learn how to "talk" to AI and get amazing results

Think of AI as a Helpful Colleague

Imagine you're talking to a really smart coworker who wants to help you. You wouldn't just say "Help me" - you'd explain what you need. The same is true with AI. The better you explain what you want, the better help you'll get.

üí°

The Golden Rule of AI

Be specific, be clear, and be polite. AI responds best when you give it clear instructions, just like a human would.

The Simple Formula for Great AI Conversations

üìù The Basic Formula:

1. What you want: "Write an email..."
2. Who it's for: "...to a job candidate..."
3. The purpose: "...to schedule an interview"
4. The tone: "...in a friendly, professional tone"

Do's and Don'ts for AI Conversations

  • ‚Ä¢ Be specific about what you want
  • ‚Ä¢ Include context and background
  • ‚Ä¢ Specify the tone or style you need
  • ‚Ä¢ Ask follow-up questions if needed
  • ‚Ä¢ Say "please" and "thank you" (it helps!)
  • ‚Ä¢ Give examples when possible

‚ùå DON'T Do These
