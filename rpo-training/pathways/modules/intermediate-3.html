The FLOW Integration Framework

Use this systematic approach to integrate AI into any workflow:

F

Find Repetitive Tasks

Identify tasks you do repeatedly that follow similar patterns. These are prime candidates for AI integration.

Examples: Writing similar emails, researching companies, creating reports, scheduling interviews
L

Link AI Tools Together

Connect multiple AI applications to create seamless workflows where the output of one becomes input for the next.

Example: AI research ‚Üí AI job description ‚Üí AI posting optimization ‚Üí AI candidate outreach
O

Optimize and Standardize

Create templates and standard prompts that ensure consistent, high-quality results across your integrated workflow.

Tip: Save your best-performing prompts as templates for reuse
W

Watch and Improve

Monitor your integrated workflows, measure time savings, and continuously refine for better results.

Metrics: Time saved, quality improvements, consistency gains, error reduction
