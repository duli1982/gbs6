Key Prompting Principles

1

Be Specific

Provide clear context, requirements, and desired outcomes

2

Set the Role

Tell the AI what role to play (recruiter, manager, etc.)

3

Include Examples

Show the AI what good output looks like

4

Specify Format

Define length, structure, and style requirements
