AI for Your Specific Role

While AI fundamentals are universal, the most powerful applications are often role-specific. This module shows you exactly how to leverage AI for your particular job function, with proven templates and techniques used by top performers.

üë• For Recruiters & Sourcers

üîç Boolean Search Generation

Generate complex Boolean search strings for any role.

Create a Boolean search string for LinkedIn to find: Role: [ROLE TITLE] Skills: [KEY SKILLS] Experience: [YEARS] years Location: [LOCATION] Exclude: Agencies, consultants Format: (title OR title) AND (skill OR skill) AND location NOT (exclude)

üìß Personalized Outreach

Create compelling, personalized candidate messages.

Write a personalized LinkedIn message to: Candidate: [NAME] at [COMPANY] Role: [THEIR CURRENT ROLE] Opportunity: [YOUR ROLE] Personal touch: [SOMETHING FROM THEIR PROFILE] Tone: Professional but friendly, 150 words max Include: Why they're a great fit, role highlights, next steps
