Proven Prompt Patterns (Copy/Paste)

1) Cinematic Explainer (Landscape, 16:9)

"Create a 25-second cinematic explainer about lab safety for biotech interns. Style: modern, optimistic; Setting: bright R&D lab; Action: a mentor demonstrates glove disposal and eyewash steps. Camera: start with a slow push-in, then a medium over-shoulder, end on a static close-up of the safety poster. Audio: calm voiceover explaining steps, subtle room tone and soft UI beeps."

2) Social Role Spotlight (Vertical, 9:16)

"Generate a 12-second vertical (9:16) clip titled 'Life as a QA Chemist'. Shots: establishing lab exterior (2s), pipetting sample (4s) with macro rack-focus, tablet readout (3s) with top-down shot, smiling wave (3s). Style: high-key, crisp; Audio: upbeat instrumental and subtle lab ambience."

3) Campus Job Fair Teaser

"15-second energetic teaser for our Budapest Hiring Day. Camera: quick whip-pans, handheld vibe; Edits: match cuts to signage and smiling teams; Text overlays: date/time. Audio: crowd ambience + rhythmic claps; no dialogue."
