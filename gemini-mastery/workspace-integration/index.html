Gemini's @ Function

Gemini's @ function in Google Workspace lets you reference content from apps like Drive or Gmail directly in your prompts. Just type @ followed by the app and keywords to pull in relevant files, emails, or info. This streamlines your workflow by enabling context-aware responses without switching tabs or copying data manually.

üì∫ @YouTube

"Summarize the key insights from this @YouTube video: [Paste video link here]."

Note: Video analysis primarily relies on available captions and audio transcripts, meaning its effectiveness may be limited for videos that convey information predominantly through visuals or non-verbal cues.

üìÅ @Google Drive

"Find all documents in @Google Drive related to 'Q4 Marketing Strategy'."

Note: Results depend heavily on the exact phrasing found in document titles and content. Documents with different terminology, even if related, might not appear.

üó∫Ô∏è @Google Maps

"Show me highly-rated Italian restaurants near @Google Maps [your current location or a specific address]."

Note: For best results, provide a specific address or landmark rather than a general area. Rankings are based on user reviews and can be subjective.

üìÖ @Google Calendar

"What's my schedule look like on @Google Calendar for next Monday?"

Note: Be specific with dates and times, using phrases like "next Tuesday at 2 PM" rather than vague terms. Include duration and attendees for complete calendar entries.

‚úÖ @Google Tasks

"Add 'Research new project management tools' to my @Google Tasks list."

Note: Always clearly state the action (add, show, complete, modify) and be specific about the task title and any associated dates/times.
