üöÄ 1.1 Overview of Gemini

Gemini is Google's advanced AI model that can understand and generate text, images, audio, video, and code. It powers tools like Search, Gmail, and Android to help users write, solve problems, and complete tasks. The latest versions offer strong reasoning skills and can even take actions like planning or making calls.

Prompt engineering is the art of designing and refining the instructions you give Gemini to achieve optimal results. A well-crafted prompt can generate high-quality outputs, reduce errors, and save time by delivering what you need on the first try.
