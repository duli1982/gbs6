üîß 2.2 Intermediate Level Prompts Showcasing Output Variety

With a single topic, the design of your prompt can drastically affect the output. Let's explore how different prompt structures create different types of responses using the same topic: Global Warming.

‚úçÔ∏è Creative Writing Prompt:

You are a creative storyteller specializing in eco-fantasy. In 5 paragraphs, write an engaging short story where global warming awakens an ancient forest spirit that intervenes to protect the Earth. Make it imaginative and awe-inspiring.
Expected Output:

Gemini will generate a story where the forest spirit emerges as a mythical protector, illustrating creative writing tailored to the topic of global warming.

üìä Factual Text Prompt:

You are an environmental scientist explaining complex topics to a general audience. In two paragraphs, summarize the primary causes and consequences of global warming in a concise manner.
Expected Output:

Gemini will provide a concise explanation of global warming, breaking down its causes and effects in clear, accessible language.

‚ùì Q&A Prompt:

You are a climate change expert helping people understand climate change at a beginner level. What are the top three causes of global warming, and what solutions can mitigate each cause? Answer each cause and solution in separate paragraphs.
Expected Output:

This format prompts Gemini to provide a structured response, detailing each cause of global warming with its corresponding solution.

üìù List Generation Prompt:

You are an environmental strategist. List and explain five key human activities contributing to global warming. Format your answer as a numbered list.
Expected Output:

Gemini will generate a detailed, numbered list identifying and explaining human activities like deforestation and fossil fuel combustion.

üí¨ Conversational Style Prompt:

You are an encouraging and friendly environmental educator. Person A: "I'm worried about the planet. What can I do to help fight global warming?" Person B: "There are lots of ways you can make a difference..." Continue this dialogue with three actionable tips for Person A.
Expected Output:

Gemini continues the dialogue with practical advice on fighting global warming, maintaining a supportive and engaging tone.

üí° Key Insight:

Using labels for elements like role, tone, task, and format can make your prompts more structured. However, Gemini is flexible and can produce relevant responses even without explicit labels. The most crucial factor is providing ample context and clear, detailed descriptions of what you need.
