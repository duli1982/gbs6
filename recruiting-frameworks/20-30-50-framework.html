How You Would Say It

Here's the everyday voice how you'd explain this to your team:

Look, Boolean and X-ray are still needed, but they're not the superpower anymore. Two, three years ago maybe. Today we've got AI tools that can build decent strings and find profiles 10x faster than we can.

Where we're actually losing or winning is not in search it's in trust. It's how we talk to people, how we show up in their inbox, and what kind of recruiter brand we build for ourselves in the market.

We don't want a team of "Boolean artists" who can't hold a human conversation. We want a team where search basics are solid, AI does the heavy lifting in the background, and humans are focused on connection, credibility, and experience.

So for us, the game is: 20% search craft, 30% AI assist, 50% human & brand.
