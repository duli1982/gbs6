üöÄ Getting Started

What is the GBS EMEA Learning Hub?
The GBS EMEA Learning Hub is a comprehensive AI training platform designed specifically for recruitment professionals. It provides:
  • Structured learning paths for AI skills (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
  • A library of ready-to-use AI prompts for daily tasks
  • Interactive workshops and simulations
  • Progress tracking with badges and certifications
All content is free and designed for GBS EMEA team members.
Do I need an account to access the content?
No account is required! All content is freely accessible. However, your learning progress and badges are saved locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:
  • No personal data is sent to servers
  • Progress is tied to your current browser
  • You can export your progress data anytime
Which learning path should I start with?
We recommend starting with the Beginner pathway (~40 minutes) if you're new to AI. It covers:
  • What AI is in plain English
  • How to write your first AI prompts
  • Practical applications for daily tasks
If you already use AI tools regularly, you can jump to Intermediate or take the AI Skills Audit to find your level.
Can I skip modules or must I complete them in order?
Modules within each pathway unlock sequentially. You must complete Module 1 before Module 2, and so on. This ensures you build foundational knowledge before advanced concepts. However, you can pause anytime and resume later - your progress is automatically saved.

üíé AI Prompts & Gemini Gems

What are Gemini Gems?
Gemini Gems are custom AI assistants you can create in Google's Gemini platform. They're like personalized chatbots trained for specific tasks. Our prompt library includes a "Generate Gem" feature that converts any prompt into a ready-to-use Gem configuration.
How do I create a custom Gem?
To create a Gem from our prompts:
  1. Visit the GBS Prompts page
  2. Find a prompt you like and click "Generate Gem"
  3. Copy the generated Gem configuration
  4. Go to gemini.google.com/gems
  5. Click "Create New Gem" and paste the configuration
  6. Save and start using your custom AI assistant!
Can I share prompts with my team?
Yes! All prompts in our library are designed for team use. You can:
  • Copy any prompt directly from the page
  • Generate a Gem and share the configuration
  • Submit your own successful prompts via the Submit Content page
Are these prompts compatible with ChatGPT?
Most prompts work with any AI platform (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.). The Gem generation feature is specific to Google Gemini, but the core prompts are platform-agnostic. We indicate in each prompt description if there are platform-specific features.

üìä Progress & Tracking

How is my progress tracked?
Your progress is tracked locally in your browser using localStorage. We track:
  • Completed modules per pathway
  • Earned badges and completion dates
  • Time spent on each pathway
  • Overall completion percentage

Important: Data is stored in your browser only. If you clear browser data or switch browsers, your progress may be lost (unless you export it first).

Where can I see my badges?
Visit the My Badges page to see:
  • All earned badges with completion dates
  • Locked badges showing what's next to unlock
  • Overall stats (completion %, modules completed, time invested)
  • Export/Import buttons to backup your progress

You can also access this from the RPO Training page by clicking "üèÜ My Badges".

Can I export my progress?
Yes! On the My Badges page, click the "Export Progress" button. This downloads a JSON file containing:
  • All completed modules
  • Earned badges with dates
  • Time spent per pathway
  • Your unique user ID

You can import this file on another device or browser to restore your progress.

What if I lose my progress?
If you haven't exported your progress, it may be lost if you:
  • Clear your browser cache/cookies
  • Use a different browser
  • Switch devices

Prevention: Regularly export your progress from the My Badges page. This creates a backup you can import anytime.

‚öôÔ∏è Technical Support

Which browsers are supported?
The Learning Hub works best on modern browsers:
  • Recommended: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari (latest versions)
  • Required: JavaScript enabled, localStorage support
  • Not recommended: Internet Explorer (not supported)

Mobile browsers work, but the experience is optimized for desktop/laptop screens.

Why isn't my progress saving?
If your progress isn't saving, check:
  • Browser settings: Ensure cookies/localStorage aren't blocked
  • Private/Incognito mode: Progress doesn't persist in private browsing
  • Browser extensions: Some privacy extensions block localStorage
  • Storage space: Ensure your browser has storage available

Test by completing a module and refreshing the page. If it still shows as incomplete, check your browser settings.

How do I report a bug?

We review all feedback and prioritize bug fixes. Thank you for helping us improve!

Where can I give feedback or suggest features?
We welcome your feedback! You can:
  • Use the Feedback Form to submit suggestions
  • Submit content ideas via Submit Content
  • Report specific issues with detailed descriptions

Your input helps shape the future of the Learning Hub!

üéØ AI Skills Audit

What is the AI Skills Audit?
The AI Skills Audit is a quick assessment that evaluates your current AI proficiency across:
  • AI Basics & Understanding
  • Prompt Engineering Skills
  • Practical Application
  • Advanced Techniques

It provides personalized recommendations for which learning path to start with.

How long does the audit take?
The AI Skills Audit takes approximately 5-10 minutes to complete. There are 12 questions covering different aspects of AI usage. You can take as much time as you need to answer thoughtfully.
Is my audit data private?
Yes, completely private. Your audit responses are:
  • Processed entirely in your browser (client-side)
  • Never sent to any server
  • Not stored or tracked
  • Only used to calculate your skill level

The AI-enhanced version (optional) uses Gemini API to provide personalized recommendations, but no personal data is included in the request.

Can I retake the audit?
Yes! You can retake the audit anytime to:
  • Reassess your skills after completing training
  • Get updated recommendations
  • Track your improvement over time

Simply visit the AI Skills Audit page again.
